Goal: Build a solid DeFi foundation.

Kick off with a free introduction to decentralized finance. Learn to set up and secure a crypto wallet (e.g., MetaMask), understand essential DeFi terms like liquidity pools and gas fees, and execute your first token swap on a decentralized exchange like Uniswap. By the end, you’ll navigate the DeFi ecosystem with confidence, ready to invest like a pro.

Week 2: DeFi Basics & Smart Contracts

Goal: Master DeFi protocols and smart contracts.

Dive into the mechanics of top DeFi platforms like Aave and Compound. Understand how smart contracts power decentralized apps and practice interacting with them safely. You’ll learn to evaluate DeFi projects for profitability and security, setting the stage for high-ROI investments.

Goal: Start generating passive income.

Learn Damian’s proven strategies for yield farming and staking to achieve up to 100% APY ROI, as seen on Spartan Capital’s results. Master providing liquidity, staking assets, and optimizing rewards while managing risks. Start building your passive income stream this week!
